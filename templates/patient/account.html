Account deletion

Delete your account

When your Quick Lab account is deleted, this will remove your access to patient Access, together with the account details we hold in relation to you. This does not affect your medical record which will remain with your GP. Please note, it may take up to 24 hours to remove any existing sessions you may have on any other devices.

Please note:

After deleting your account you will not be able to access your account by signing in with NHS login. You’ll need to contact your GP practice to request that they reset your online registration, and issue a new letter to you. You'll need to provide proof of your address and identity. You can then re-register for Quick Lab at any time and re-link to your GP practice with the new registration letter or by using NHS login.
Please refer to our Privacy policy  for more information.
