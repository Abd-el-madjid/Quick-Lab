How do I access my lab test results?

Lab results are delivered to your QuickLab® Patient™ portal account. Log in or register online. For more information, you may also see our Notice of Privacy Practices.

Can I view my past lab results on QuickLab® Patient™ portal?

Yes, QuickLab® Patient™ portal allows you to access and view your past lab results. You can conveniently review your historical data and track any changes over time.

How long does it take for lab results to be available on QuickLab® Patient™ portal?

Lab results are typically delivered to your QuickLab® Patient™ portal account within a specified timeframe. The turnaround time may vary depending on the type of test performed. If you have any specific concerns, please contact our support team
